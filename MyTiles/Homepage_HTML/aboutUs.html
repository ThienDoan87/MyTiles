About Us

THE STORY OF EURO TILE & STONE

In 1987, founders Ben and Sandra Colasanti identified a gap in the industry while Ben was working as a tile setter. They experienced customer service that didn’t go beyond the simple transaction, and product selection was extremely limited. The Colasantis thought the industry deserved better and were inspired to do more.

Together they established Euro Ceramics (the precursor to Euro Tile & Stone).

30+ years later, not only has the business expanded to a team of over 75, but it has also managed to maintain the warm, personalized service that Euro Tile & Stone has become known for. While Ben and Sandra often refer to Euro Tile & Stone as their first child, they are thrilled to have the second generation playing a key role in the business.

Principles

Our four guiding principles are to treat clients like family, only sell high-quality products you believe in, have plenty of on-hand inventory, and hire people who share our passion and values.
