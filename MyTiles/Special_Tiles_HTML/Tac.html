Tactiles are unique non-fade, non-slip ground surface indicators (vitrified tiles). These indicators provide cues, which combined with other environmental information, assist visually challenged people with their orientation. Johnson Endura Tactile collection has been designed with an emphasis on reliability, safety, dignity, and independence for visually challenged people.
